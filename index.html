Metric Translator

Plain-English definitions for the dashboard.

Turnaround

The time between one plane landing and that same plane taking off again.

Tight turn

A turnaround under 45 minutes. It can be efficient, but there is less room to recover from delays.

Idle turn

A turnaround over 180 minutes. The plane is sitting for a long time between flights.

Delay propagation

A late plane arrives, then its next flight also leaves late. This is delay spreading.

Recovery rate

A late plane arrives, but the next flight still leaves on time. This is the operation catching up.

Airborne hours

How many hours the average plane spends flying in a day. More flying usually means better aircraft use.
